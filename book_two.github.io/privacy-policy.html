Privacy Policy for FlipBook Pro

FlipBook Pro ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices when you use our digital journaling application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Full name (first name)
  • Profile avatar (if provided)
  • Password (encrypted)

Journal Content

Your written journals and creations are stored in two locations:

  • Local Storage: On your device's browser (private)
  • Cloud Storage: On Supabase servers (only if you save to cloud)

Usage Data

We may collect non-personal analytics about how you use FlipBook Pro, including:

  • Features you use
  • Pages created and edited
  • Time spent in the application
  • Errors encountered (for debugging)

Device Information

We may automatically collect technical information:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (for security)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain FlipBook Pro
  • Authentication: To securely verify your identity
  • Cloud Sync: To save your books to the cloud when requested
  • Improvements: To enhance features and fix bugs
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud or misuse
  • Communication: To send updates or support messages (with your consent)

3. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

Your journal entries are stored in your browser's local storage. This data:

  • Remains on YOUR device only
  • Is NOT transmitted to our servers
  • Is deleted if you clear your browser cache
  • Is NOT encrypted (your device's security is your responsibility)

Cloud Storage

If you choose to save books to the cloud, your data is stored on Supabase (supabase.co):

  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
  • We use Supabase's security infrastructure
  • Only YOUR account can access your books
  • You can delete cloud data anytime in Privacy Settings

Password Security

Passwords are:

  • Encrypted using industry-standard algorithms
  • Never stored in plain text
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never visible to FlipBook Pro staff

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However:

Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate FlipBook Pro:

  • Supabase: Cloud database and authentication
  • EmailJS: For newsletter subscriptions (only if you opt-in)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (optional)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights, including:

  • Compliance with court orders
  • Preventing fraud or illegal activities
  • Protecting the safety of users

Business Transfers

If FlipBook Pro is sold or merged, your data may be transferred as part of the transaction. You will be notified of any change in data practices.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics or communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at garrickkogola@gmail.com

6. Cookies & Tracking

FlipBook Pro uses:

  • Session Cookies: To maintain your login session
  • Local Storage: To save your preferences
  • NO Third-Party Trackers: We do not use Google Analytics or similar services by default

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

FlipBook Pro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will delete such data and terminate the child's account.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored on Supabase servers, which may be located outside your country. By using FlipBook Pro, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries other than your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide FlipBook Pro:

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Journal entries: Retained until you delete them
  • Analytics data: Retained for 12 months then aggregated
  • Backup copies: Retained for 30 days after deletion

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or by posting the updated policy on FlipBook Pro. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have privacy concerns or questions, please contact us:

  • Email: garrickkogola@gmail.com
  • GitHub: github.com/GRK-WEK

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights
